Resolves all ways and nodes that were changed in this changeset to segments (connections of two nodes). Then returns three lists of segments: 1. Those that have been removed (which is the case even if one of their nodes has been moved) 2. Those that have been created 3. Those that havenâ€™t been changed but have somehow been affected by the changeset, be it by changing their tags or by changing another part of the way they belong to. Nodes that have been changed will be represented by a Segment consisting of two equal Nodes.

Returns:
